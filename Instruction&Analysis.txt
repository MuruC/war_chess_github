Instruction

Click Recruitment button to buy soldier
Click Soldier unit
Green grids around unit are where soldier can go. One Move cost one steps
Knight, Cannon, Tank cannot go to lake or mountain
Cow grids add HP. Gold grids add money
Click Attack button to attack
Different types of soldiers have different attack distance
Click Shield Button to defense
Defence action will transform all steps into block.
Click Up Arrow Button to level up. Add Max HP or attack
Level Up costs XP. One unit can only be upgraded for once.
Click Sell button to sell the unit
The goal is to arrive at opponent's castle.
